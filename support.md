For TransparentBLE support, bug reports, or privacy questions, open an issue on GitHub:

[https://github.com/arturpilavetz/transparentble-app/issues](https://github.com/arturpilavetz/transparentble-app/issues)

When reporting a problem, include:

- iPhone or iPad model
- iOS version
- TransparentBLE app version
- BLE device model or name, if relevant
- Steps to reproduce the issue

Do not post private BLE payloads or sensitive diagnostic logs publicly unless you are comfortable sharing them.
